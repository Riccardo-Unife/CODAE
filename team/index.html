01 // Principal Investigator

Prof. Maria Rossi

Full Professor — University of Ferrara

Maria Rossi leads the CODAE research group at the Department of Architecture of the University of Ferrara. Her work focuses on computational methods for structural optimization and adaptive systems, bridging advanced numerical simulation with architectural design. She holds a PhD in Structural Engineering from Politecnico di Milano and has been visiting researcher at ETH Zurich and TU Delft.
